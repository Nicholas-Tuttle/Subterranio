Subterranio - Gleba adds the Gleban biospheres, a peaceful area full of life. Bacterial colonies are in a constant cycle with fungal growth, growing and decaying one after the other. This area opens research opportunities for new freezing technologies and packaging recipes to improve the efficiency and consistency of long logistic chains.
